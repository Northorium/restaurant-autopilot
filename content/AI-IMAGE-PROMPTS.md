## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be specific** - Include lighting, angle, mood
2. **Use food photography terms** - "hero shot", "close-up", "overhead"
3. **Add atmosphere** - "moody", "dramatic", "warm", "cozy"
4. **Include style** - "street food", "fine dining", "rustic"
5. **Generate 4 at a time** - Pick the best one

## Export Settings

- Instagram Post: Square (1:1) or Portrait (4:5)
- Instagram Story: Portrait (9:16)
- YouTube Thumbnail: Landscape (16:9)
